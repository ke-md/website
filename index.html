MettāDhura

MettāDhura is an experiential homestay built in a quaint little sub-alpine Himalayan village Merdhura in Jalna, Almora, Uttarakhand. An ideal place for those looking for a break from the humdrum of our daily city lives, take some time off to reflect, meditate, read, write and replenish energies. One can immerse in the local life and culture with forest trails around the place, visits to the local markets and villages nearby.

Cuisine

Bon Appétit

Indulge in exceptional Kumaoni cuisine. It’s a blend of modern kitchen setup and traditional Kumaoni cuisine. Ingredients are locally sourced. It can accomodate sixteen guests at a time with two different seating arrangements – traditional floor pillow seating and contemporary table seating. Enjoy the beautiful views of the Himalayas and forest around while dinning. Bon Appetite, is also created as a multi-use space, open to performances and musicals.
