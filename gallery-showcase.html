Modern & Unique Image Gallery Designs

A showcase of five different responsive gallery styles for a vacation rental website.

Design 1: The Masonry Grid

Like Pinterest, this layout is ideal for mixing vertical and horizontal photos without cropping, creating a dynamic and engaging flow.

Design 2: Fullscreen Slider

An immersive, full-page experience where one image fills the screen at a time with elegant text overlays, perfect for storytelling.

Click the button below to open the fullscreen slider.

Design 3: Thumbnail Filmstrip

Features a large main image with a scrollable strip of thumbnails below, allowing for easy and intuitive navigation between photos.

Design 4: Justified Grid

A very clean and organized look where images are resized to fill all horizontal space, forming perfect, edge-to-edge rectangular rows.

Design 5: Interactive Grid

A standard grid where hovering over an image reveals a subtle animation and a title, adding a layer of interactivity before opening the lightbox.
